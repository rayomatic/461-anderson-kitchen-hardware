Treats the sitting cabinet as a furniture piece (like an armoire) — 4 knobs converging toward the horizontal divider creates a classic furniture pattern.

Design rationale

  • Brand: Emtek Satin Brass (US4) — switched from original Top Knobs Honey Bronze to better match Grohe Minta faucet (Brushed Cool Sunrise). Consolidates with Emtek door hardware (Orb knob in ORB) already specified for the home.
  • Two-collection family: Paxton round knob (Timeless Classics) for upper doors and the sitting cabinet — transitional, warm, universally readable. Trail bar pulls (Modern Collection) for drawers and lower-cabinet vertical pulls. Identical Satin Brass finish unifies them.
  • 1/3 sizing rule applied to drawers/doors: 3" pulls on 12"-wide drawers (3DB12), 5" on small pull-outs (B12-PULL, B18-PULL), 8" on standard drawers (1DB27, 3DB27, OVD2484 bottom drawers) and base-cabinet vertical doors (BBC, SB30, B15R), 12" on tall pantry doors.
  • Placement: drawer pulls centered; lower-door vertical pulls at the top (natural reach); pantry pulls mid-height (~38–50" off floor); upper-door knobs at lower-inside corner; sitting cabinet knobs at upper-inside corner.
  • Considered and rejected: vertical pulls on navy uppers (too busy); Trail T-Knob (too modernist); all-pulls scheme (too contemporary); 5" verticals on upper doors (stubby/crowded).
