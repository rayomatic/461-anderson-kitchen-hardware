Design rationale

  • Brand: Emtek Satin Brass (US4) — chosen to match Grohe Minta faucet (Brushed Cool Sunrise). Consolidates with Emtek door hardware (Orb knob in ORB) already specified for the home.
  • Cadet + Warwick family: Cadet 1" Mushroom Knob for upper doors and the sitting cabinet; Warwick handle pulls for drawers and lower-cabinet vertical doors. Cadet's softer 1" mushroom reads as period-appropriate furniture hardware; Warwick is a more substantial bar profile that holds visual weight against the navy lowers. Identical Satin Brass finish across all kitchen hardware.
  • 1/3 sizing rule applied to drawers/doors: 3-1/2" pulls on 12"-wide drawers (3DB12), 5" on small pull-outs (B12-PULL, B18-PULL), 8" on standard drawers (1DB27, 3DB27, DO2410625 bottom drawer) and base-cabinet vertical doors (BBC, SB30, B15R), 12" on tall pantry doors.
  • Placement: drawer pulls centered; lower-door vertical pulls at the top (natural reach); pantry pulls mid-height (~38–50" off floor); upper-door knobs at lower-inside corner; sitting cabinet knobs at upper-inside corner.
  • Considered and rejected: vertical pulls on navy uppers (too busy); Paxton/Westridge (Timeless Classics — too geometric for the warm palette); Trail (Modern Brass — too contemporary); all-pulls scheme (loses warmth); 5" verticals on upper doors (stubby/crowded).
